Observatory houses
Categories
competitions, architecture
Location
Roccascglena, Italy
Year
2017
Description
Architecture is all about geometry and light. The light is about perceiving the form and the darkness is because the absence of light. Having that in mind, the new intervention itself is a clear strong gesture that complements and amplifies with the impressive landscape. The new white stripe is clearly set apart from the fortress, so it is evident that they are built at different periods of time. The stripe that meanders and adapts to the ground becomes dramatic as the terrain itself, which imitates the way of building of the old fortress where each wall follows the natural shape of the rock, forming different platforms with a beautiful view. The gesture starts at the edge of the old town and ends with a tower that interprets the old tower which collapsed in 1940. An inner street connects the programs from the entrance of the building and at some points it opens to the outside and leads to the external extensions such as: observatory swimming pool, observatory amphitheater and various platforms for observing the landscape or camping. Curiosity for exploring and the feeling of mystery are awaken with the play of light and shadows. The promenade culminates in a tower which contains a water collector with an observation telescope at the top and is connected to the fortress with a folding bridge. The houses are places of solitude and daydreaming, representing observatories on their own way. They are archaic shelters with carefully selected openings so that visitors can peek into the landscape or observe the starry sky. Their program is minimal in order to focus on their main goal, the chance to watch the sky in an extremely intimate atmosphere.
